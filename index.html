BLACKGROUND

The Center for Renewable Energy and Climate Actions (CRECA) was founded in March 2023 when five like-minded friends met online with a shared passion for climate action and sustainability. Since then, CRECA has grown into a platform for advocacy, innovation, and community empowerment, driving change in clean energy, agriculture, WASH, and natural resource governance.
CRECA is a leading organization working to build a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future. We promote climate change adaptation and mitigation by empowering communities with knowledge, tools, and practices that reduce environmental impact. Our work includes raising awareness and encouraging the adoption of renewable energy technologies, while also advocating for policies and incentives that speed up the transition to clean energy. Beyond energy, CRECA supports sustainable agriculture to strengthen food security, advances WASH initiatives to improve access to safe water and sanitation, and champions transparency in the extractive sector to ensure natural resources benefit everyone. Guided by integrity and accountability, we are committed to creating lasting change that uplifts communities and protects our environment.
CRECA has a multi-disciplinary team with advanced degrees in Soil Science; Climate Change and Sustainable Development, Finance, Environmental Science, Engineering, Management, and Renewable Energy, coupled with practical field experiences in community mobilization, farmer training, and climate justice advocacy.
